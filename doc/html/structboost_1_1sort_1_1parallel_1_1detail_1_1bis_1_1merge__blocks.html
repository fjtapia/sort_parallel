from a big range of positions of blocks in the index. Examine which are mergeable, and generate a couple of ranges for to be merged. With the ranges obtained generate function_t elements and are inserted in the concurrent stack. When the range obtained is smaller than Group_size, generate a function_t calling to merge_range_pos, when is greater, generate a function_t calling to cut_range

Parameters
rposrange_input : range of the position in the index, where must extract the ranges to merge
